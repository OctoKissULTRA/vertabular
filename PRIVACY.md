# Privacy Policy

Vertabular is a personal, open-source project. 

I don't collect your data. I don't have a server. 

### How your data is handled:
- **Tabs & Bookmarks:** Used only to make the extension work.
- **Storage:** Settings and Workspaces stay in your browser or your Google Sync account.
- **Tracking:** There is none.

If you have questions, open an issue on the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/OctoKissULTRA/vertabular).
